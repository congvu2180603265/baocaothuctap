> Here, you can collect feedback about your personal site, your work, or anything you want visitors to share with you.

### Suggested Prompts

**1. What kind of content would you like to see here?**  
Leave ideas for posts, projects, notes, or other sections you want to add.

**2. Which page is most useful?**  
Tell me which section you visit most often and what could be improved.

**3. What should be improved?**  
Share anything that feels confusing, incomplete, or unnecessary.

**4. Would you like this site to be more portfolio-like, blog-like, or documentation-like?**  
That helps shape the content structure later.

**5. Any other comments?**  
Use this space for free-form feedback.
